Musings of Asutosh Palai

My Nursery School

I joined Guru Nanak Public School for my nursery education.

Though the memories of my first school is hazy, yet they are presiously preserved! How I used to cry all the day to go home! Innocence! No care for anything else! Where was this competing world then??

My High School

I did my schooling at St. Vincent Convent School at my hometown, Berhampur.

As it happens with everyone, I have never forgotten my old School. I still walk down the lanes of old memories of that enchanted place with elven friends.

My Senior Secondary School

After 10th I went to Bhubaneswar, capital of Odisha, for my coaching and 12th board.

I joined Buxi Jagabandhu English Medium School for senior secondary studies. The memories of this school are still fresh in my mind. It is this school that gave me the required platform to jump to a whole new world.

My present Institution

After JEE Advancedem>, I reached IIT Roorkee, the birth place of Indian Engineering.

My new dwelling!! It is a different world and different level to be here. I am mesmerised!
